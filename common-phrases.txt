# Common phrases - Haüfige Redemittel

Haüfige - Frequent
Common - Verbreitet
Redemittel - Speech
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Danke / Danke schön - Thanks
2. Vielen Dank! - Thank you very much
3. Bitte / Bitte schön - You are welcome (Bitte has multiple meanings, its meaning vary according the place where it is used)
4. Bitte - Please

1. Entschuldigen sie, bitte - Excuse me Please
2. Entschuldigen - act of telling excuse/apology 
3. Entschuldigen sie/du/dich (Ich) - You Excuse/apologise (me) [In this cases 'ich' need not be used, as the excuse word itself means to excuse me, since excuse whom is not specified default it referes to person who tells this]
4. Entschuldigen sie, bitte - (In english we tell excuse me, but in german if Entschuldigen alone used it refers the word apologize but who should apologise is by default is the person to whom he is telling, but whom to be apologized to be mentioned in german)
5. Entschuldigung bitte - Excuse me please (Informal situation)
6. Entschuldigung - Sorry
7. Es tut mir leid - I am Sorry

1. Wie bitte - I beg your pardon
2. noch einmal bitte - Once again please/ repeat once/ repeat one more time
3. Bitte wiederholen sie - Please repeat (you)

1. Sprechen sie Deutsche / Englische ? - Do you speak german or english
2. Ich spreche kein Deutsche - I don't speak german
3. Ich sprece englische - I speak english
4. Ich spreche etwas Deutsche - I speak a bit of german

1. Ich verstehe nicht - I don't understand
2. Ich weiß nicht - I don't know
3. Keine Ahnung - No idea
4. Kein problem - No problem
5. Einen moment, bitte - Just a moment please/ One second please

2. Nein - No
